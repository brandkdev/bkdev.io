About

My programming journey began in childhood, sparked by a fascination with how computers work. I started by coding mini-games in Lua on a kid-friendly online platform, where I developed a passion for creating interactive experiences.

As I grew older, I initially pursued a path in mechanical engineering, but I soon realized that my true calling lay in programming and software development. In 2021, I reignited this passion by diving into The Odin Project, an intensive course that introduced me to web development. Through this program, I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby, building a solid foundation in modern web technologies.

Eager to deepen my knowledge, I transitioned to a more traditional education and enrolled in the online Computer Science program at the University of Southern Mississippi. Here, I have greatly expanded my skillset, mastering languages like C++ and assembly, as well as diving into database design and systems programming. I am on track to graduate in May 2025.
